How to choose

First-time cruiser guide

The four questions that determine which cruise line is right for you.

01
Who's travelling?

Families with children do best on Royal Caribbean or Norwegian. Couples wanting romance should look at Princess or Celestyal. Solo travellers thrive on Norwegian, which has a dedicated single-cabin programme.

02
What's your budget?

MSC and Celestyal offer the strongest value — from $349 per person for a full cruise holiday. Royal Caribbean and Norwegian sit in the mid-range. Princess cruises start higher but typically include more.

03
How long to sail?

Short 3–5 night breaks work best on Royal Caribbean from Florida. Classic 7-night cruises suit every line. For 14+ nights, Princess and MSC offer the most routing options.

04
Ship size matters

Large ships have more facilities but visit busy ports. Smaller ships like Celestyal reach quieter harbours. Mid-size ships are the safest, most versatile choice for first-time cruisers.
